**Hook (0-3s)**: 
"Thinking about a tattoo but scared to commit? Watch this 👀"

**Problem (3-8s)**:
Shows person looking nervous at tattoo parlor, scrolling through designs

**Solution (8-20s)**:
- Opens AI Tattoo Preview app
- Selects dragon tattoo
- Points camera at arm
- Captures photo
- Drags tattoo into position
- Resizes it
- Shows realistic preview

**CTA (20-25s)**:
"Try it FREE at [link in bio]"
