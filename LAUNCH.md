## Emergency Pivots

**If no traction after Week 2**:
1. Post in tattoo-specific communities
2. Reach out to tattoo influencers
3. Offer free custom design consultations
4. Partner with one studio for case study

**If high traffic but no conversions**:
1. UX audit (record user sessions)
2. Simplify onboarding
3. A/B test pricing
4. Add more free value

**If legal issues arise**:
1. Add stricter disclaimers
2. Consult IP lawyer re: tattoo designs
3. Get insurance (Hiscox, Embroker)
